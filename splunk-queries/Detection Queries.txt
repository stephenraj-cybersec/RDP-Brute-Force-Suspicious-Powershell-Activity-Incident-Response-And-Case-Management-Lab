Detection Queries :
1)Failed Login Detection
index=* EventCode=4625
Purpose: Detect failed authentication attempts indicating possible brute force activity.

2)Successful RDP Login Detection
index=* EventCode=4624 Logon_Type=3
Purpose: Detect successful logins.

3)Encoded PowerShell Detection
index=*  "*-enc*"
Purpose: Detect Base64-encoded PowerShell execution, often used to evade detection.

4)PowerShell Download Cradle Detection
index=* "DownloadString" "IEX"
Purpose: Detect PowerShell download cradle activity used to retrieve remote payloads.

5)PowerShell Logs 
index=* EventCode=4104
Purpose: Detect all PowerShell Logs.

6)Logout Logs
index=* EventCode=4634
